Mindfulness

Mindfulness

Mindfulness is being present. It is about being aware of your mind and body as well as your surroundings. You are aware of the thoughts and feelings you may have but are not letting them overwhelm you. It will help with your stress levels and in situations where you feel anxious.

Mindfulness techniques

Here is a list to help you practice mindfulness:

  • Focus on your breathing.
  • Going for walk.
  • Listening to relaxing music.
  • Eating healthy.
  • Body scan practice.

What next?

If you like these techniques, then we recommend listening to podcasts such as 'Mindfulness Meditation Podcast' and 'Mindfulness for Beginners'. These have helped thousands of people with advice and helpful tips.
